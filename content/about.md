I am a cloud and O-RAN infrastructure engineer based in Taipei.

My background is in system integration for open radio networks. Before my M.Sc. at NTUST,
I spent three years at the Telecom Infra Project Community Lab in Bandung deploying
OpenRAN, OpenWifi, and OpenTransport stacks with live operators.

My thesis built an automated CI/CD testing framework for O-RAN using the O2 infrastructure
management interface, deployed on StarlingX and OpenShift. I contributed the NFO and FOCOM
modules to the O-RAN Software Community's PTI-O2 project via the Gerrit review process.

I work primarily with Kubernetes, Python, Go, and Linux. The infrastructure problems I find
interesting sit at the intersection of open networking standards and real deployment
constraints.

**Open to roles in:** cloud infrastructure, DevOps/platform engineering, network engineering.
Singapore and remote considered.
